About Polyglot Speech Therapy

Led by Komal Dhokiya, Registered Speech-Language Pathologist (R.SLP), BC, Polyglot Speech Therapy provides compassionate, evidence-based services tailored to children and families from diverse linguistic and cultural backgrounds.

Komal holds a Bachelors in Audilogy and Speech Language Pathology from India and Postgraduate Diploma in Autism and Behavioural Science from Ontario and has over 8 years of clinical experience supporting children with a wide range of communication needs.

She specializes in pediatric speech and language therapy, with a strong focus on supporting children with Autism ADHD, speech and language delays, and neurodevelopmental differences through individualized, family-centered care.
